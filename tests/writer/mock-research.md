# Faceless Tech Channel Research — Instagram

## Source: Exa web search + manual review
## ADMIRALTY Grade: B2

### Key Findings

1. **@techinsider.ai** (245K followers) — Posts AI tool tutorials as carousels. 3-5 posts/week. Avg 2.1% engagement rate. Monetizes via affiliate links to SaaS products. Niche: AI tools.

2. **@cyberdaily** (89K followers) — Cybersecurity news in reel format. Daily posts. 1.8% engagement. Uses fear-based hooks ("Your phone is being watched"). Niche: Cybersecurity.

3. **@ai.money.moves** (156K followers) — AI + investment crossover. Carousels with financial projections. 2x weekly. 3.2% engagement. Niche: AI/Finance.

4. **@dev.shortcuts** (312K followers) — Coding tips, IDE shortcuts, tool reviews. Mix of reels and carousels. Daily. 2.8% engagement. Niche: Developer tools.

5. **@claude.updates** (34K followers) — Anthropic/Claude focused content. New feature announcements, tutorials. 3x weekly. 4.1% engagement (high for niche). Niche: Vendor-specific.

### Patterns Observed

- Carousel format outperforms reels for educational content (2.5% vs 1.9% avg engagement)
- Channels posting 3-5x/week outperform daily posters on per-post engagement
- Fear/urgency hooks drive initial views but hurt long-term follower retention
- Vendor-specific niches (Claude, GPT, Cursor) have smaller audiences but 2x engagement rates
- Most successful channels cross-post to TikTok with format adaptation (carousels become slideshows)
